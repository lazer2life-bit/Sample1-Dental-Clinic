Got Questions?

We Have Answers

Do you see children?
+
Absolutely! We love treating children and recommend their first visit around age 1. Dr. Kim is our dedicated paediatric specialist and is brilliant with nervous young patients.
Are your treatments painful?
+
We use the latest anaesthetic techniques and always take the time to ensure you're completely comfortable before we begin any treatment. Pain-free dentistry is our standard, not our exception.
What are your payment options?
+
We accept all major cards and offer 0% interest payment plans for treatments over £500. We also offer monthly membership plans starting from £19/month covering your regular care essentials.
How quickly can I get an appointment?
+
We typically have availability within 48 hours for new patients. For emergencies, we maintain same-day slots. Book online 24/7 or call us during opening hours.
Do you offer whitening for sensitive teeth?
+
Yes! We offer specially formulated whitening treatments for sensitive teeth that are just as effective but much gentler. We'll assess your sensitivity at your consultation and recommend the best approach.
Is parking available?
+
Yes, we have dedicated free parking for patients directly outside the practice. There is also a large public car park 2 minutes' walk away with discounted validation we provide.
