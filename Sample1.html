Questions Answered

Frequently
Asked Questions

How long do porcelain veneers last?
+
With proper care, Lumière's porcelain veneers typically last 15–20 years. Our veneers are crafted from the highest-grade feldspathic porcelain, which resists staining and maintains its translucency over time.
Is the treatment process painful?
+
We use advanced numbing techniques and premium anaesthetics to ensure complete comfort throughout every procedure. Most patients report minimal to no discomfort, and many fall asleep during treatment.
Do you offer payment plans?
+
Yes, we partner with premium finance providers to offer 0% interest payment plans for up to 24 months. Our patient coordinators will walk you through all available options during your consultation.
How many appointments will I need?
+
Treatment timelines vary by procedure. Whitening can be completed in a single session, while a full smile makeover typically spans 3–5 appointments over 4–8 weeks. We provide a detailed timeline at your consultation.
What makes Lumière different from other clinics?
+
We treat dentistry as a form of craftsmanship. Every detail — from the custom shade matching to the precise margin fit of each veneer — is executed with the same rigour as a master atelier. We never rush, never compromise.
